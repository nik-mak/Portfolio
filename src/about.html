About Me

Hi, I’m Nik. I am currently a student at Coder Academy doing the Web Dev Fast Track course.

I am going through a bit of a career change at the moment. Previously I was an Instrument Chemist and Lab Technician at Eurofins Scientific, working in the environmental section. I found that I wasn’t feeling quite as happy working in a lab as I do when I am behind a screen. Wanting a dynamic and challenging career, Web Development was the only reasonable conclusion. What started out as a hobby, playing around with HTML and CSS, has now become my goal.

My one and only true love in this world is travel. I love going to regional Victoria (which in my opinion is way underrated) and experiencing nature and the local towns. Unfortunately, I haven’t done a lot of international travel. I have only been to Singapore and Bali but Japan is, and will always be, my ultimate destination.

I believe in making your own future, and I cannot wait to see where I’ll end up!
